Answers

Frequently asked questions

Can't find what you need? Email us — we reply to every message.

How do I scan a document?
Tap Scan Document on the home screen, line up your page, then capture. Adjust the crop corners and tap Use Crop to get a clean PDF page. You can also Import Photo from your library.點首頁「掃描文件」，對準頁面拍照，拖曳四角微調裁切後「使用此裁切」即可；也可「匯入照片」。
Is it really 100% offline?
Yes. Scanner Pro makes zero network requests. Everything — scanning, OCR text recognition, search, PDF export — runs on your device. It works exactly the same in Airplane Mode.是。本 App 不發出任何網路請求，掃描、OCR、搜尋、匯出全在裝置上；開飛航模式也一樣。
Where are my documents stored?
Privately on your device, inside the app's own storage. They are never uploaded to any server and we never see them. Use Save to Files or Save to Photos if you want a copy elsewhere.只存在你裝置上 App 的私人空間，永不上傳；想另存可用「存到檔案／照片」。
How does searching the text work?
After each scan, on-device OCR reads the text automatically. Just type a word in the search bar and Scanner Pro finds every document that contains it. Tap View Text to read or copy the recognized text.每次掃描後 OCR 自動辨識文字，搜尋列輸入關鍵字即可找到內含它的文件；「檢視文字」可閱讀/複製。
How do I turn on the Face ID lock?
Open Settings → App Lock and turn it on. The app will then require Face ID or your device passcode each time it opens.到「設定 → App 鎖定」開啟，之後每次開啟 App 都需要 Face ID 或裝置密碼。
How do I export an encrypted (password) PDF?
Open a document, tap the ••• menu and choose Export Encrypted PDF. Set a password — it will be required to open the file. Keep it safe; it cannot be recovered.開啟文件 → 「•••」→「加密匯出 PDF」，設定密碼（無法復原，請保管好）。
Is this a subscription?
No. Scanner Pro is a one-time purchase. Every feature is included forever — no subscription, no ads, no in-app purchases.不是。一次買斷，所有功能永久包含，無訂閱、無廣告、無內購。
I changed phones / restore my purchase
Reinstall Scanner Pro from the App Store while signed in with the same Apple ID, then tap Restore Purchases if prompted. Your purchase is tied to your Apple ID. Documents are stored on-device, so back up via iCloud or export to Files before switching phones.用同一 Apple ID 重新下載即可；文件存在裝置上，換機前請先用 iCloud 備份或匯出到檔案。
Which languages are supported?
English, 繁體中文, 日本語 and 한국어 — auto-detected from your device, or switch any time in Settings. OCR recognizes text in all four.支援英／繁中／日／韓，自動偵測或在設定手動切換；OCR 四語皆可辨識。
